Welcome to Our Family Website

About Our Family

The Jamesville Family of Adukrom Akuapem is a proud lineage with deep roots in Ghanaian culture and tradition. Our family has contributed significantly to the development of our community and continues to uphold the values passed down through generations. We are spread accross all the Regions of Ghana in different continents in the Wolrd. You are welcome to share your pictures and stories to this platform. Our aim is to connect all the decendants of Opanyin James Kwadjo Larbi of Adukrom.

Family Building Project

Updates on our multipurpose family house construction in Adukrom.

Project Overview

3-story multipurpose family house with meeting halls, guest rooms, and museum

Current Status

65% complete - Structure finished, now working on interiors

Project Timeline

Started: July 2024

Expected completion: December 2025

Project Details

  • Location: Jamesville Family Land, Adukrom
  • Total Cost: GH₵500,000
  • Amount Raised: GH₵325,000 (65%)
  • Project Manager: Arch. Kwabena Osei
  • Contractor: Asante Construction Ltd.

Progress Photos

How to Contribute

You can support this project through:

  1. Cash donations (see Donation page)
  2. Material donations (cement, roofing sheets, etc.)
  3. Volunteer labor
  4. Professional services (architectural, electrical, etc.)

Contact the Project Committee at building@jamesvillefamily.org or call 0244-112233 for more information.
